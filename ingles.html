ELETRO WATER

The project consists of a fan propeller that, when moved, rotates a DC motor responsible for converting mechanical energy into electrical energy that can be stored and used at a future time, whether it is necessary or economical












DC motor

A DC motor works by converting electrical energy into mechanical movement, but it is possible to generate electrical energy using mechanical movement. It does this by means of an internal coil that interacts with a magnet, generating an electromagnetic field which in turn generates an electrical current. Not all DC motors are suitable for this application, as efficiency varies from motor to motor.



Fan propeller

In this project we are simulating an application for a mechanical energy converter (wind or water), where we can save on electricity bills and reduce the impact on the environment through the use of sustainable energy.



Applications

In our project we simulated a load with LEDs but we can store this energy in batteries to use in an emergency or in cases where the battery is already fully charged apply this energy indoors with lamps, cell phone chargers or other simple loads.

















Hydroelectric

We can use this same principle of energy generation in the plumbing of a building where the force generated by gravity itself on the water is able to turn the propeller, generating electrical energy that can be stored and then used in emergency lights in a building



Renewable energy

The importance of sustainable energy and the impacts of non-renewable energy are crucial issues for our world. Sustainable energy plays a key role in preventing climate change and preserving the environment. It is obtained from sources such as solar, wind and hydroelectric power



Non-renewable energy

On the other hand, non-renewable energy, such as fossil fuels, is responsible for a series of negative impacts. Burning these resources releases large amounts of carbon dioxide and other atmospheric pollutants, contributing directly to global warming and air pollution. In addition, the extraction of oil and coal often results in significant damage to ecosystems, soil degradation and other problems.
